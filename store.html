About

Purpose

Sam and Karen are best friends and currently go to middle school. In their free time, the one game they love to play is Rock Paper Scissors. THey always place bets with each other to see who wins and they play veyr long games, sometimes best of 30 rounds. Overtime, they found that regular Rock Paper Scissors was simply too boring for them, and so they added two new choices, a lizard, and a spock, each of which has their own strengths and weaknesses.
