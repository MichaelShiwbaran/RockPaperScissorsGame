About Rock, Paper, Scissors:

Rock Paper Scissors was originally invented in China and believed to be created around the time of Christ. It stayed only in China until the 1700s where it spread to Japan, and then the rest of the world.

Rules of Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock:

The rules of this game are quite simple. You pound your closed fist into your hand three times while chanting "rock-paper-scissors," then signal your choice by making a fist to represent "rock," pointing your index and middle fingers to represent "scissors," or opening your hand wide to represent "paper." As for lizard, you play this by pressing your thumb upward against the other 4 fingers, and a spock is created by displaying your entire hand while separating your middle and ring fingers to create a gap.
